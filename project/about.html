Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order? +

Simply browse our catalog, add books to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can pay securely with various payment methods.

What is your return policy? +

You can return books within 14 days of purchase if they are in original condition. Contact our support team for assistance.

Do you offer international shipping? +

Currently, we ship within the country. International shipping options are coming soon!

How can I track my order? +

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use it to track your package on our website.

Do you have physical stores? +

Yes! We have 15 branches across major cities. Visit our contact page for store locations and hours.
